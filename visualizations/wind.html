Wind Speed Graph

This graph explores the relationship between latitude change (bi-directionally away from the equator) and wind speed. Negative latitude denotes southerly deviation and positive latitudes denote northerly deviation. This graph of wind speeds is heavily bottom concentrated which should come as a relief to most humans; no one wants to be whipped around by winds of 15mph or higher on a daily basis. It is interesting to note that the cities with higher wind speeds tended to be further away from the equator.
