Cloudiness Graph

This graph explores the relationship between latitude change in the northerly direction away from the equator. The fitted line indicates that despite the seemingly randomly distributed points, the data shows that cities tend to be cloudier the further you travel away from the equator. This graph could help someone decide whether or not a new location for a home is suitable for them based on cloudiness.
